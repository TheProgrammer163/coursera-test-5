What we offer

The problem with universities

When learning a language, in school, colleges or universities, it's not always enough. We don't practice speaking the language we learn that often.

We don't learn informal speech

The focus is not on informal speech, so we end out talking like robots. We don't speak the same way with friends than we do in a professionnal setting. If we do not learn to speak informally, we cannot speak naturally.

Univerisites teach too slow. They aren't enough.

You write a text, your Spanish teacher takes it but has a ton of copies to correct. A week or two later you see your results and the mistakes you made.
This is a slow process. When practicing speaking, this is once in while. It isn't a 1 on 1 where you only speak Spanish. It isn't as intense and effective as speaking with a native speaker of that language, only in that language, for 30 minutes in a row.

Phone Calls or Video Calls in Spanish

One service we offer is a phone call or video call, using a software like Skype, Whatsapp or Facebook Menssenger. The goal of this is to speak almost entirely in Spanish the entire time. One of the best thing you can do to learn a language is to immerse yourself in it. Without travelling, this is hard to do. With a phone or video call you can practice in a similar way.

Speaking is essential to become fluent. Don't try to become fluent so that you can speak, speak so that you become fluent. You will improve much faster.

Teaching Strategies

Beginners

If you're are a beginner or if you haven't learned enough vocabulary yet, we have a plan.

  • We will help you choose a subject (it could be a book or a movie, something simple but effective)
  • We will help you write a text of what you will talk about. Telling a story can be easier than having a conversation. Similar to reading texts, in the sense that you will already know all the verbs and words needed to tell about the subject you chose.

Advanced Learners

If you have basic speaking skills, we have a plan

  • Informal, friendly conversation as if it were between friends
  • We will help you choose a subject, book or movie you liked to talk about
  • You will use verbs and vocabulary you already know to speak

Advanced beginners to advanced

In this case the conversation should be more similar to a conversation between friends. Asking questions back and forth, talking about different subjects.

We will help you choose a subject like a book or a movie. The difference is we won't make you prepare a text beforehand. In this case you will use the vocabulary and verbs you already know.
